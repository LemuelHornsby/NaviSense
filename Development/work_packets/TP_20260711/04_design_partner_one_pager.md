## The offer

**NaviSense Design-Partner Pilot** — 8 weeks, fixed scope, €8,000–€15,000 (scoped to your vessel and
scenario complexity; quoted after a 30-min scoping call).

We build, together:
1. **Your vessel profile** — hull particulars into the MMG maneuvering model; IMO turning-circle and
   zig-zag KPIs computed for your vessel, not a stand-in.
2. **Your scenario suite** — the COLREGS encounters (or other maneuvers) relevant to your
   operational picture, run in photoreal Monaco or another supported port.
3. **Your evidence report** — a scored, repeatable report (IMO KPIs + rule-conformance verdicts +
   plots) in the format a class-society or regulator assurance case actually wants.
4. **Roadmap influence** — pilot feedback shapes the public roadmap; your use case becomes a
   (anonymized, with permission) reference case.

- Closed-loop Python control of a CFD-grounded vessel through photoreal Monaco, live since mid-June.
- Full sensor suite (GPS/IMU/AIS/radar/camera) validated against the physics plant.
