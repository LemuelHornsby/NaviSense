Counterparties: each member of the ship-design group who contributed to the DOLPHIN hull design (and, if the design was coursework, mind the university dimension in the companion letter).

## Cover note (email)

Dear [names],

As you know I've been building a marine simulator, NaviSense, that uses the DOLPHIN design we created together as its demonstration vessel. I'm now taking it commercial under my company, NaviSyn Marine Solutions, and I want our positions documented fairly and early — so there's never ambiguity, and so you're credited properly. Attached is a short consent/license. Happy to discuss any of it, including attribution or a goodwill arrangement if the DOLPHIN-derived work ever generates material revenue.

## Consent and license (one page)

THIS CONSENT is given by [name], contributor to the "DOLPHIN" vessel design ([project/course, institution, year]), to NaviSyn Marine Solutions ([registration no.], "the Company").

1. **Grant.** The contributor grants the Company a non-exclusive, perpetual, irrevocable, worldwide, royalty-free license to use, reproduce, modify, and create derivative works from the DOLPHIN hull design — including its geometry, lines plan, 3D models, hydrostatic and hydrodynamic data derived from it — for the development, demonstration, validation, and commercialization of the Company's simulation software and services.
2. **What is NOT granted.** No rights to build physical vessels from the design; no exclusivity; the contributor retains all rights to use the design for any other purpose, including academic publication.
3. **Attribution.** The Company will credit the design group as "[agreed credit line]" in product documentation where the DOLPHIN appears.
4. **Warranty.** The contributor confirms they are entitled to grant this license for their contribution and knows of no third-party claim over it.
5. **Goodwill clause (optional — discuss).** [e.g., If cumulative revenue directly attributable to DOLPHIN-derived deliverables exceeds €[X], the parties will agree a one-time recognition payment of €[Y] / the Company will donate €[Y] to [department]. Delete if keeping it purely royalty-free.]
