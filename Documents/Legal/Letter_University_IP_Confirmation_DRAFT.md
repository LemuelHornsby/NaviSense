# DRAFT — University IP Confirmation Request

**Status: DRAFT for your edit. Not legal advice — have a lawyer or the university's tech-transfer office confirm wording matters if the response is anything but a clean confirmation.**
Send from your company address (once live), or personal university email. Attach nothing initially.

---

To: [Supervisor / Department head / Technology Transfer Office]
Subject: Confirmation of IP position — [thesis title], [programme, year]

Dear [name],

I completed my master's thesis "[title]" in [programme] at [university] in [year], supervised by [supervisor]. Since graduation I have independently and substantially extended the software originating from that work into a commercial simulation product (working name NaviSense) under my company, NaviSyn Marine Solutions.

Before taking on commercial pilots and grant funding, I want to have our respective positions documented cleanly. Could you please confirm in writing:

1. That under [university]'s IP regulations applicable to taught/master's students in [year], the copyright and other IP in software and documents I authored for the thesis vest in me as the student (or, if not, what the university's position is);
2. That the university asserts no claim over the post-graduation extensions and derivative works I have developed independently since [date];
3. Whether any third-party terms (funding attached to the project, lab resources, supervisor contributions) attach conditions I should know about.

If any of 1–3 is not a straightforward yes, I would welcome a short call with the appropriate office to agree a simple confirmation or license on standard terms. I am keen to keep [university] as a friendly reference and potential research partner — this is housekeeping ahead of commercial diligence, not a dispute.

Thank you and best regards,
Lemuel [surname]
[company, address, date]

---

**Fill before sending:** thesis title/date, programme, supervisor, the university's actual student-IP policy (read it first — many vest software IP in taught students by default, which makes this a one-line confirmation). **File the reply in this folder.**
